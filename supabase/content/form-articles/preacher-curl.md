# Preacher Curl

## Setup
- Adjust the preacher bench so the upper arms rest flat on the pad with armpits near the top edge.
- Sit firmly with chest against the pad; feet flat on the floor.
- Grip the bar or dumbbells with an underhand grip; arms extended but not hyperextended at the elbow.

## Execution
- Curl the weight up by flexing the elbows; keep the upper arms glued to the pad.
- Squeeze the biceps at the top without lifting the elbows off the pad.
- Lower with control to full extension; avoid bouncing at the bottom.
- Exhale on the way up; maintain a neutral wrist position.

## Common mistakes
- Lifting elbows off the pad — reduces isolation and shifts load to the shoulders.
- Swinging or using hip drive — keep the torso still against the bench.
- Hyperextending elbows at the bottom — stop just before lockout to protect the joint.
- Wrists bending backward — keep wrists in line with the forearms.

## Safety notes
- Use a weight you can control through full range without elbow strain.
- Stop for sharp elbow or forearm pain.
- Consult a qualified coach or clinician if you have prior elbow or biceps tendon issues.
