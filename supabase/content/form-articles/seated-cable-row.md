# Seated Cable Row

## Setup
- Attach a V-bar or handle to a low cable; sit with feet on the platform, knees slightly bent.
- Grab the handle; sit tall with a slight lean back from the hips, arms fully extended.
- Brace the core; shoulders relaxed, chest up.

## Execution
- Pull the handle toward the lower chest or upper abdomen; drive elbows back close to the body.
- Squeeze the shoulder blades together at the end of the pull.
- Extend the arms forward with control; allow a slight stretch without rounding the spine.
- Keep the torso angle consistent; avoid rocking excessively back and forth.

## Common mistakes
- Using momentum and swinging the torso — reduce weight and pull with the back.
- Shrugging shoulders on the pull — depress the shoulders and lead with the elbows.
- Rounding the lower back at full stretch — maintain a neutral spine throughout.
- Pulling too high toward the chin — aim for the lower chest or upper abs.

## Safety notes
- Choose a load that allows a full stretch and squeeze without losing posture.
- Stop for sharp lower-back or biceps pain.
- Consult a qualified coach if you have a history of lower-back issues.
