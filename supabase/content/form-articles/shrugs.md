# Shrugs

## Setup
- Stand with feet hip-width; hold dumbbells or a barbell at the sides or in front of the thighs.
- Arms hang straight; shoulders relaxed down, chest up, core braced.
- Grip firmly; avoid bending the elbows during the movement.

## Execution
- Elevate the shoulders straight up toward the ears in a vertical line.
- Hold briefly at the top; squeeze the upper traps without rolling the shoulders.
- Lower the shoulders with control to a full stretch at the bottom.
- Keep the neck neutral; do not jut the chin forward.

## Common mistakes
- Rolling the shoulders in circles — move straight up and down only.
- Bending the elbows — arms stay straight throughout.
- Using momentum from the legs or torso — keep the body still.
- Shrugging with the neck — isolate the traps, not the neck muscles.

## Safety notes
- Avoid excessive load that causes neck strain or grip failure.
- Stop for sharp neck, shoulder, or upper-back pain.
- Consult a qualified coach or clinician for prior neck or cervical spine issues.
