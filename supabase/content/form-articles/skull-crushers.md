# Skull Crushers

## Setup
- Lie on a flat bench with feet flat on the floor.
- Press the bar or EZ bar to arms-length over the upper chest or slightly behind the head.
- Grip shoulder-width (EZ bar) or with hands inside the outer bends.
- Pin the upper arms in place; elbows point toward the ceiling or slightly back.

## Execution
- Bend the elbows to lower the bar toward the forehead or just behind the head.
- Keep upper arms stationary; only the forearms move.
- Extend the elbows to return to the start; squeeze the triceps at lockout.
- Lower with control; avoid dropping the weight onto the forehead.

## Common mistakes
- Flaring the elbows wide — shifts stress to the shoulders and reduces triceps work.
- Letting the upper arms drift forward or back during the rep — breaks isolation.
- Using a weight that forces elbow flare or bouncing — reduce load.
- Partial range — aim for a full stretch at the bottom without shoulder pain.

## Safety notes
- Have a spotter or use a moderate load when training near failure.
- Stop if you feel sharp elbow, wrist, or shoulder pain.
- Consult a qualified coach if you have a history of elbow tendinopathy.
