# Walking Lunge

## Setup
- Stand tall holding dumbbells at sides or a barbell on the back (if experienced).
- Brace the core; look forward; feet hip-width before stepping.

## Execution
- Step forward into a lunge; front knee tracks over the foot, not past toes excessively.
- Lower until the back knee approaches the floor; torso upright.
- Drive through the front foot to step through or return to standing.
- Alternate legs or complete all reps on one side before switching.

## Common mistakes
- Short steps — front knee travels too far forward.
- Torso collapsing forward — reduce load and shorten stride.
- Back knee slamming the floor — control the descent.
- Wobbling — reduce weight or shorten range until stable.

## Safety notes
- Clear space for the walking path; avoid obstacles.
- Stop for sharp knee or hip pain.
- Consult a qualified coach if balance is a limiting factor.
