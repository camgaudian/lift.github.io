# Pallof Press

## Setup
- Attach a cable or band at chest height; stand sideways to the anchor with feet hip-width.
- Hold the handle at the center of the chest with both hands; step away until there is light tension.
- Stand tall with knees slightly bent; brace the core and glutes.

## Execution
- Press the handle straight out in front of the chest, arms fully extended.
- Hold briefly against the rotational pull; keep hips and shoulders square to the front.
- Return the handle to the chest with control; resist rotation throughout.
- Complete all reps on one side, then switch stance and repeat.

## Common mistakes
- Rotating the torso toward the cable — the goal is to resist rotation, not follow it.
- Shrugging shoulders or locking the knees — stay relaxed but braced.
- Pressing too fast — use a slow, deliberate tempo for better core engagement.
- Standing too close to the anchor — increase distance for appropriate tension.

## Safety notes
- Choose a load you can control without twisting or losing balance.
- Stop if you feel sharp lower-back or hip pain.
- Consult a qualified coach if you are recovering from a core or spinal injury.
